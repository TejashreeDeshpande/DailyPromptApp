# Feature: Word Frequency Analyzer

The product team wants to build a word frequency analyzer.

Users should be able to type or paste a block of text into an input field. The app should
display the top 10 most frequent words and how many times each appears, sorted from most
to least frequent. Common words such as "the", "a", and "is" should be excluded from the
results. The analysis should update live as the user types.
