# Feature: Animated Splash Screen

The product team wants to build a branded splash screen with an entrance animation.

When the app launches, users should see a splash screen displaying the app logo and name.
The logo should animate into view (e.g. fade in, scale up, or slide in — your choice).
After the animation completes, the screen should automatically transition to the main screen.
The transition itself should also be animated. The splash screen should not be shown again
if the user simply navigates back to the app from the background.
