# Feature: Favorites List

The product team wants to build a favorites feature.

Users should see a list of items (e.g. books, movies, or recipes — your choice). Each item
should have a favorite toggle button (such as a heart or star icon). Tapping the toggle
should mark or unmark the item as a favorite. Users should be able to switch between
viewing all items and viewing only favorited items via a filter control at the top of the screen.
